Interests

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

I was always fascinated of the idea to build systems that perform a specific task like a human, or even better cause of the computational power of the computers. Moreover, I liked the idea of agents that can learn from data different patterns and to become better in course of time. Thus, I wanted to "dive" deeper into the field of AI and Machine Learning in order to learn how these can be used into various settings, like self-driving cars etc. in order to make the life of people easier.

Data Science & Analytics

Having to derive interesting facts and insights from various data might not be considered a very challenging task, but in fact it is very useful in many fields. For instance, businesses nowadays hire Data Analysts, in order to find patterns in data targeting to increase their sales by knowing the needs of their clients. Moreover, Explanatory Data Analysis is very useful to Machine Learning, because it helps us make a more effective model.

Full-Stack Web Development

Knowing that Full-Stack web development was one of the most on-demand jobs ,surrounded everywhere by webpages and by knowing that when you should make a product you should also deploy it through a website I took the decision to start learning various web development technologies both in back-end and in front-end. The main reason for that was to have a spherical knowledge about web development and to know in depth how a website works.
